# Google Form templates

## Theme voting (multiple choice grid)
- **Respondent name:** Short answer
- **Select up to 6 themes:** Checkbox list of all themes from themes.json
- **Optional comments:** Paragraph
- **Close date:** Set manually (one week before first event)

## Dish sign-up
- **Name:** Short answer
- **Event date:** Dropdown (list second Saturdays)
- **Dish name:** Short answer
- **Cuisine/region:** Short answer
- **Vegetarian/non-veg:** Multiple choice
- **Allergens present:** Checkbox (nuts, dairy, gluten, shellfish, egg, soy, sesame, other)
- **Portion size:** Number (serves)
- **Recipe link or note:** Paragraph

## Hosting sign-up
- **Name:** Short answer
- **Preferred event date(s):** Checkbox (second Saturdays listed)
- **City & state:** Short answer
- **Max capacity:** Number
- **Parking & notes:** Paragraph

- **Best dish vote:** Short answer (or multiple choice of dish names if prefilled)
- **What went well:** Paragraph
- **What could be improved:** Paragraph
- **Photos link (optional):** Short answer

## Best dish vote (standalone)
- **Event date:** Dropdown
- **Select best dish:** Multiple choice (populate from sign-up list)
- **Why you chose it (optional):** Paragraph
