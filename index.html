Theme-based fun events

Each event includes a short activity to keep it playful and memorable.

  • "Taco Tuesday" Trivia

    Test your knowledge of Mexican culture and food facts.

  • "Italian Night" Gelato Making

    Hands-on session to create a custom sweet treat.

  • "Southern Comfort" Charades

    Act out famous Southern movies, songs, or phrases.

  • "Asian Fusion" Chopstick Challenge

    Race to pick up tricky items with chopsticks.

About the club

We’re friends from Kerala, living across the US, keeping our food, stories, and laughter alive through themed potlucks. Everyone brings a dish, shares recipes and memories, and hosts when they can. Family-friendly, zero-pressure, maximum joy.
